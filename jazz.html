About Us

We are your new favorite local jazz band that brings the classic and modern sounds of jazz to life. From smooth swing to toe-tapping funk, our music will create an unforgettable experience for any atmosphere.


The Pelham Street Trio members are proud alimni of Notre Dame Colledge School where they started their love for jazz. As they gigged around the niagara region and participated nation-wide in MusicFest Canada winning many awards, this local supergroup continues to share their love for music around Niagara
