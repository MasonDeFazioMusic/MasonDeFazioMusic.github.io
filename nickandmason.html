Background Music for Any Occasion

From smooth jazz cocktail hour to funky backyard parties, the Nick Fraraccio and Mason De Fazio duo has music for any occasion. Having played music together since they were 13 years old, Nick and Mason have a strong repertoire of tunes. Nick and Mason have also been part of the Notre Dame Jazz Combo for four years and competed in provincial-wide music festivals. Check out our socials and demo videos below, and if you like what you hear, do not hesitate to contact us to discuss booking.
